## How to Use These Scripts

These ASMR scripts are designed to be flexible and adaptable to your creative needs. Whether you're a voice artist, content creator, or simply enjoy reading scripts for personal relaxation, you're welcome here.

### For Voice Artists

- Feel free to interpret the scripts in your own unique style
- Sound effect suggestions are marked with *[brackets]* and are optional
- Pacing and pauses are at your discretion
- You may adapt the script to better suit your voice and recording style

### For Personal Use

- Read scripts aloud for your own relaxation
- Use them as sleep aids or meditation guides
- Share recordings with friends and family (non-commercial)

## License & Usage Rights

{:.license-box}

### ✓ You MAY:

- Use scripts for personal ASMR recordings
- Monetize your recordings on platforms like YouTube, Patreon, etc.
- Make minor adaptations for flow and personal style
- Create multiple recordings of the same script

### ✗ You MAY NOT:

- Claim authorship of the original script
- Sell or redistribute the raw scripts themselves
- Use scripts in AI voice generation without explicit permission
- Remove or alter attribution

## Attribution Requirements

When using these scripts, please include:

**At minimum:**
- "Script by Souris Ray"
- Link to this website: [sourisray.github.io](https://sourisray.github.io)

**Preferred attribution:**
- Include the script title
- Link to the specific script page
- Mention in your video description or audio notes

### Example Attribution:

> *"[Script Title]" written by Souris Ray.*
> *Script available at: [link to script page]*

## Terms & Conditions

### Respect & Community

- Scripts are provided in good faith for the ASMR community
- Respect other creators who may also use these scripts
- Maintain a positive and supportive community environment

### Script Modifications

Minor edits for clarity, timing, or personal preference are acceptable. Major rewrites should be discussed.

### Commercial Use

Monetization of your ASMR recordings is permitted and encouraged. However, the scripts themselves may not be sold separately.

### Content Warnings

Some scripts may contain themes or scenarios not suitable for all audiences. Review scripts carefully before recording.

### Revocation

I reserve the right to revoke usage rights for any creator who:
- Violates attribution requirements
- Uses scripts for harmful or malicious purposes
- Engages in harassment or toxic behavior

---

## Questions?

If you have questions about usage rights, adaptations, or collaborations, please reach out via Discord. I'm happy to discuss specific cases and grant additional permissions where appropriate.
